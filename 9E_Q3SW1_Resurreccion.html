Bungou Stray Dogs


SYNOPSIS

After Nakajima Atsushi, was abandoned by his orphanage, he lived on the streets not wanting to starve to but also lived in fear of the tiger roaming around Yokohama that he believed to be after him. After saving Dazai Osamu from his drowning suicide attempt, Atsushi assists him and his partner Kunikida Doppo in their investigation for the tiger, only to find out its true identity was...

CHARACTERS

Nakajima Atsushi

The series's protagonist, after being abandoned by the orphanage he struggles to find permission to continue living, only when he joined the Armed Detective Agency did he face hardships that help him find that permission. A boy who's a little shaky but fierce when fighting.

Ryunosuke Akutagawa

A young man who's the Port Mafia's main mercenary for his ability named "Rashomon", he's introduced attempting to injure and capture Atsushi to gain the 7 billion reward on his head for the Port Mafia. He lived in the slums of Yokohama with his sister, Gin Akutagawa, since childhood and joined the PM as young teens.









Osamu Dazai

The one who invited Atsushi to join the Armed Detective Agency (ADA) after discovering the tiger's identity, he makes frequent suicide attempts but often failing at them. He considers committing suicide alone "so last century" and wants to commit a lover's suicide with a beautiful woman.
